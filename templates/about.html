About Me

This is what I do.

Hi! I'm Adebowale Abisolami, and I've been right where you are—feeling lost after high school, unsure about what's next. But guess what? I found my way, and now I'm here to help you do the same.

I remember the pressure to have all the answers, but let's be real, it's tough. I talked to my parents, mentors, and teachers. Those conversations changed everything. It led me to tech, and now I'm on a journey to become a data scientist. Trust me, the path isn't always straight, but it's worth exploring.

Whether you're still in high school or fresh out of it, I've got your back. I'm not just another adult telling you what to do. I'm a student too, and I know the questions that keep you up at night. We'll dive into stories, strategies, and advice from people who've been there. Together, we'll find your path.

I'm also a programming enthusiast. The thrill of turning lines of code into something real—it's pure magic. And that's what I want for you too, to find joy in your journey, to explore your interests, and to take those uncertain steps toward an exciting future.

Thanks for joining me on this adventure. Let's turn confusion into clarity, one step at a time.
